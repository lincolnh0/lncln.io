## Motivation
I've always been a fan of the show Countdown and have wondered how well I'd do if I was on the show. As a vibe-code exercise, I have built a prototype of an interactive web app that simulates the game show using Nextjs and managed to find a couple of online resources to look up the best possible solutions.

## Vision
The project can easily be extended and translated into other alphabet based languages provided that the distribution of popular letters are properly weighted. All that is lacking now is the iconic tune, mascot reveals and a witty host to complete the presentation.
